I remember the first time we talked for hours without realizing it. The way your eyes light up when you talk about your dreams, how you crinkle your nose when you're confused, the sound of your laughter that can brighten my darkest days – even through a screen, you've managed to capture every piece of my heart.

Happy 1 Month Anniversary, my love. Here's to the thousands of messages we've exchanged, the countless hours of sleep we've sacrificed just to hear each other's voice, and the beautiful future we're building together.

I can't wait for the day when I can finally hold you in my arms and tell you all these things in person. Until then, know that every beat of my heart whispers your name.

Forever yours,
Dhruv ❤️
